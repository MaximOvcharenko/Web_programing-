About

Cinque Terre, meaning “Five Lands,” is a string of five picturesque seaside villages perched on the rugged cliffs of the Italian Riviera. These colorful towns—Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore—are connected by scenic hiking trails and offer breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea.
